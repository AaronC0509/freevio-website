Our Story

Our Story

Freevio is a young brand, which was founded in July 2019 by three friends and was supposed to be named Coffee Cup because of their common interest in coffee. The trio soon realized they also have another thing in common; the passion in bringing immersive and new technology to the masses and thus after overcoming many hurdles, Freevio was born. Since its inception, Freevio has been providing its clients’ end-to-end technology services and solutions. The company’s steady growth in its early stages is attributed to clients coming back for more, through marketing by word of mouth and its promise to be one of the best solutions provider in the market.
